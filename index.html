More About Me

I am Owaise Zarger. I come from a small place Latur in Maharashtra and I am currently living in Pune. I graduated in B.Tech Computer science engineering in 2019 from MIT, Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad.

After graduation, I started teaching BSc students in a college. I taught subjects like operating systems, software engineering, HTML, and CSS. I worked there for 2 years and then joined Mphasis as a technical support engineer, where I worked for alight process and resolved issues through calls and email.

While working in Mphasis I came across Masai school, which offers 30 weeks of full-time full-stack web development course, where I learned frontend and backend technologies.
